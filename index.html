About Us

Prathibha Vidyalaya, established in 1991, is a renowned educational institution dedicated to providing quality education to students. It was founded by Mr. Ravindra Reddy, who took on the role of the correspondent and has been the guiding force behind the school’s success. Under his leadership, Prathibha Vidyalaya has grown to become one of the most respected schools in the region.

The school is now led by our present Principal, Mrs. Bommi Milton, whose vision for educational excellence and holistic development has taken the school to new heights. With a focus on both academic and co-curricular growth, Prathibha Vidyalaya continues to shape the future of its students, fostering a love for learning and a commitment to societal well-being.

Admissions for 2025-2026 Batch

Prathibha Vidyalaya is now accepting offline applications for the academic year 2025-2026! We are committed to providing quality education and an all-round development experience to our students. Admissions are open for all grades, and we invite parents and guardians to apply and be part of the Prathibha Vidyalaya family.

Admission Process

The admission process for the 2025-2026 batch follows a structured procedure to ensure fairness and transparency:

  • Step 1: Visit the school office to obtain the admission form.
  • Step 2: Submit the completed admission form along with the required documents at the school office.
  • Step 3: The school will schedule an interview for prospective students, if required, depending on the grade level.
  • Step 4: Upon successful selection, parents will be notified, and the admission process will be completed.

Required Documents

Please ensure you submit the following documents for the admission process:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous Year Marksheet (if applicable)
  • Address Proof
  • Parent’s ID Proof
  • Passport-sized Photographs
